About

Tedly is a campus planner built for students who have a lot going on and not a lot of time to keep track of it all. Instead of juggling sticky notes, group chat reminders and a dozen half finished to do lists, everything lands in one place.

Whether it is a summative assignment due monday, a quiz worth more than it should be, or just something small you do not want to forget, Tedly gives it a home on your dashboard so you can see what is coming up at a glance.

The idea behind it is simple. School already asks enough of your attention. Tracking your own deadlines should not be the hard part.
